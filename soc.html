The Problem

The Security Operations Challenge

Running an effective SOC is one of the most resource-intensive challenges in enterprise security. Most teams are losing the battle before it begins.

🔴
Analyst Burnout & Alert Fatigue
Security teams are overwhelmed — the average SOC receives 11,000+ alerts per day. Analysts manually reviewing each alert miss critical threats buried in the noise. Burnout leads to slower response times and costly analyst turnover.
🔴
24/7 Coverage Gaps
Maintaining true 24×7 coverage requires 3+ analyst shifts, significant headcount, and continuous training investment — with skill gaps emerging during nights and weekends when attackers know teams are thinnest.
🔴
Escalating Dwell Times
The industry average dwell time for a breach is 197 days. By the time an analyst reviews an alert, attackers have already established persistence, moved laterally, and exfiltrated data. Speed is everything — and humans can't keep up.
🔴
Talent Shortage & High Attrition
The global cybersecurity workforce gap exceeds 3.4 million professionals. SOC analysts have an average tenure of 18 months, creating constant knowledge loss, onboarding costs, and institutional memory gaps that weaken your defenses.
