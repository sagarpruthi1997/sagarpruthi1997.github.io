The Challenge

Why Enterprise Data Protection Keeps Failing

Sensitive data is everywhere — and growing exponentially. Without AI-powered discovery, classification, and protection, organizations are defending data they cannot even find, against threats they cannot detect in time.

🔍
Data You Don't Know About
The average enterprise has 1,000+ data stores — structured, unstructured, cloud, and SaaS. Without discovering all data locations, you cannot protect or govern what you cannot find. Shadow data in forgotten S3 buckets, SharePoint sites, and legacy databases represents your largest unmanaged risk surface.
🏷️
Overclassification & Underclassification
Manual data classification is inaccurate and exhausting. Too strict and productivity suffers; too loose and sensitive data flows unprotected through email, USB, and cloud applications. Rule-based classifiers miss context — flagging innocuous documents while missing genuinely sensitive combinations of data that together create PII exposure.
🌍
Privacy Regulation Complexity
GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, PDPA, LGPD — each with different requirements for consent, retention, breach notification, and data subject rights. Managing these manually across a global organization is impossible. Regulatory fines can reach 4% of global annual revenue under GDPR — the risk of non-compliance is existential.
📤
Data Exfiltration
Insiders and external attackers target sensitive data. Without real-time DLP controls, sensitive files leave your environment through email, USB, cloud sync, and print channels before anyone notices. 43% of breaches involve insider threat or negligence — making insider-aware DLP controls as critical as perimeter defenses.

What We Provide

End-to-End Data Security & Privacy Capabilities

From discovering your first shadow data store to automating GDPR data subject requests — CloudSufi's data security program delivers comprehensive protection for your most valuable asset, at enterprise scale, across every environment.

🗺️
Data Security Posture Management (DSPM)
AI-powered discovery and continuous monitoring of all data stores — databases, file shares, cloud buckets, SaaS applications — with real-time risk scoring for sensitive data exposure. DSPM gives you the foundational visibility that every downstream data security and compliance decision requires.
  • Automated multi-cloud data discovery (AWS, Azure, GCP, SaaS)
  • Sensitive data inventory, mapping, and lineage tracking
  • Data flow visualization and cross-boundary analysis
  • Real-time risk scoring for data exposure and access risk
  • Shadow data identification and governance enrollment
🤖
AI-Powered Data Classification
Machine learning models trained on your organization's data automatically classify information at rest and in motion — far more accurately and efficiently than manual tagging or rule-based classifiers. Contextual classification understands meaning, not just patterns — dramatically reducing false positives while catching what rules miss.
  • PII, PHI, PCI, and intellectual property classification
  • Custom classification schema and taxonomy support
  • Automatic label propagation and inheritance
  • Contextual classification (semantic understanding, not just pattern matching)
  • Classification accuracy dashboards and continuous improvement reporting
🚧
Data Loss Prevention (DLP)
Endpoint, network, and cloud DLP controls that prevent sensitive data from leaving your authorized environment — blocking, alerting, and quarantining based on data classification and user context. User-aware policies distinguish between legitimate business use and exfiltration — reducing alert fatigue while maximizing protection coverage.
  • Endpoint DLP (print, USB, email, web upload, screenshot)
  • Network DLP (SMTP, HTTP/S, FTP, instant messaging)
  • Cloud DLP (OneDrive, Dropbox, Google Drive, Box)
  • User context-aware policies with behavioral baselining
  • Encrypted data inspection and SSL/TLS decryption
🔏
Privacy Program Management
End-to-end privacy program management — data subject request (DSR) automation, consent management, privacy impact assessments (DPIA), and records of processing activities (ROPA). CloudSufi transforms privacy compliance from a manual burden into an automated, continuously monitored program that scales with your organization.
  • Automated DSR / DSAR fulfillment workflow (GDPR, CCPA)
  • Consent management platform deployment and integration
  • DPIA templates, risk assessment workflow, and approvals
  • Records of Processing Activities (ROPA) maintenance and reporting
  • Privacy by Design consulting and architecture review
🔑
Data Access Governance
Who can access sensitive data, who is accessing it, and is that access appropriate? AI-driven data access governance identifies toxic access combinations, enforces least-privilege data access, and continuously certifies that access remains appropriate as roles and responsibilities change across your organization.
  • Sensitive data access mapping and permission visualization
  • Least-privilege access enforcement and right-sizing
  • Access anomaly detection and behavioral alerting
  • Data owner assignment and stewardship workflow
  • Periodic access certification campaigns with automated remediation
🔐
Encryption & Key Management
Data encryption strategy, implementation, and managed key management services — ensuring sensitive data is protected at rest and in transit across all environments. CloudSufi designs encryption architectures that protect data without creating operational complexity or performance bottlenecks that impede legitimate business operations.
  • Encryption strategy design and architecture documentation
  • Key Management Service (KMS) deployment and operations
  • Database and file-level encryption assessment and implementation
  • TLS/SSL certificate lifecycle management and rotation
  • Encrypted backup validation and recovery testing
