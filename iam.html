The Identity Problem

Identity Is the New Attack Surface

Traditional perimeter security is dead. Attackers don't break in — they log in. Every credential, every privileged account, every dormant service account is a door waiting to be opened.

🎯
Credential-Based Attacks Dominate
80% of data breaches involve compromised credentials. Stolen passwords, phishing, and credential stuffing are the most common attack vectors — and the hardest to defend without proper IAM.
📈
Privilege Creep
Users accumulate excessive permissions over time through job changes, project access grants, and forgotten temporary permissions. This privilege creep creates massive attack surface for insider threats and external attackers.
🕵️
Lack of Visibility
Most organizations have no real-time visibility into who has access to what, who is accessing it, and whether that access is appropriate or anomalous. What you can't see, you can't protect.
🔗
Fragmented Identity Ecosystem
Employees, contractors, cloud workloads, APIs, and service accounts each have separate identity silos — creating ungoverned access paths that attackers routinely exploit to move laterally across your environment.

What We Provide

End-to-End IAM Services

From privileged access management to zero-trust architecture, CloudSufi delivers every layer of identity security your enterprise requires — powered by AI and operated 24/7.

🔐
Privileged Access Management (PAM)
Full lifecycle management of privileged accounts — just-in-time access, session recording, credential vaulting, and privileged session monitoring for all administrative access across your environment.
  • Just-in-time privileged access
  • Session recording and monitoring
  • Credential vaulting and rotation
  • Privileged access certification
🏛️
Identity Governance & Administration (IGA)
Automated user lifecycle management — from joiner to mover to leaver — with access certification campaigns, role-based access control, and separation of duties enforcement to eliminate excess privilege.
  • Automated provisioning & de-provisioning
  • Access certification campaigns
  • Role-based access control (RBAC)
  • Segregation of duties (SoD) enforcement
🤖
User & Entity Behavior Analytics (UEBA)
AI continuously analyzes user behavior patterns to detect anomalies — impossible travel, off-hours access, mass data downloads, and lateral movement — flagging threats in real time before damage occurs.
  • Behavioral baseline profiling
  • Anomaly detection and scoring
  • Insider threat detection
  • Automated risk-based response
📱
Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) & SSO
Adaptive MFA deployment across all applications with risk-based step-up authentication — balancing security with user experience through contextual access policies that challenge only when risk is detected.
  • Adaptive and risk-based MFA
  • Single sign-on (SSO) across apps
  • Passwordless authentication options
  • Hardware token and biometric support
🛡️
Zero-Trust Network Access (ZTNA)
Replace legacy VPN with identity-aware, application-level access control — granting minimum necessary access to specific resources based on verified identity, device health, and contextual signals.
  • Application-level access control
  • Device posture assessment
  • Continuous access validation
  • Micro-segmentation integration
⚙️
Service Account & Non-Human Identity Management
Automated discovery, classification, and governance of service accounts, API keys, OAuth tokens, and machine identities — the fastest-growing and least-governed identity category in the enterprise.
  • Automated service account discovery
  • Secrets management integration
  • OAuth and API key governance
  • Non-human identity lifecycle

AI-Powered IAM

CloudSufi AI Agents Work While You Sleep

Our identity AI agents don't just alert — they act. Behavioral baselines are built automatically. Anomalous access is blocked in milliseconds. Risky accounts are flagged for review before damage can occur.

🧠
AI-Powered Behavioral Baselines
Machine learning builds a unique behavioral fingerprint for every user and entity — detecting deviations from normal access patterns that no static rule would ever catch.
📊
Continuous Access Risk Scoring
Every access request receives a dynamic risk score based on identity, device posture, location, time, and behavior — triggering step-up authentication only when risk warrants it.
⚡
Automated De-Provisioning on Anomaly
When an AI agent detects a high-risk behavioral anomaly, it can automatically revoke access, terminate sessions, and escalate to analysts — reducing breach window from hours to seconds.
🎯
Context-Aware Adaptive Authentication
Authentication decisions adapt to real-time context — a user logging in from their usual device at their usual time is treated differently than the same credentials used from an unknown location at 3 AM.
